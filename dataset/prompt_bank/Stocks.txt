This dataset consists of historical daily stock market data in candlestick format for a constituent stock of the S&P 500 index. Candlestick charts are commonly used in technical analysis to represent price movements over time, displaying the open, high, low, close, volume and adjusted close prices for each trading period. Each data point consists of over 300 technical indicators derived from the candlestick data, and is valuable for analyzing historical price trends and building predictive models.
